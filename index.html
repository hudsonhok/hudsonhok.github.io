Problem Statement: College Student Budgeting

Many college students across all universities and colleges in the nation have trouble keeping track of their spending, which can lead to mounting fees, overwhelming debt, and lesser quality of life. This mismanagement of funds stems from improper organization of expenses as well as inappropriate spending.
